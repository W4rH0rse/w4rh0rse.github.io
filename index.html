Welcome!! I'm W4rH0rse and I help people unlock the phantom bow and DICE LA camo. On this page you will find information on how to find Final Stand map dogtags, unlock your phantom assignments and get the bow.

I have listed the steps needed to unlock the bow in three sections: TAGS, ASSIGNMENTS, ELEVATOR.


1 - Getting Final Stand Tags


The way we find the dogtags is to search all possible spots until we find one. This page has an excellent up to date map of the possible locations, only missing one glitched location on Hammerhead: https://imgur.com/a/hzBRN

They are in tiny boxes about the size of a box of kitchen matches. The boxes are white on Operation Whiteout and Hammerhead maps, and green with a brown lid on Giants Of Karelia and Hangar 21 maps.

The boxes will slightly glow in IRNV/PLD scopes if your within approx 40 meters of it. They do not glow at all in FLIR/MAV sights, they used to but a patch changed that.

The Final Stand dogtags can spawn at 1 of 30 (1 of 31 on Operation Whiteout) possible spots. It will ONLY spawn at 1 spot at a time. They do not spawn instantly on the start of a new round. The very first spawn in a round seems to be variable between 5-10 minutes.

We usually start looking 8 minutes after a round starts. By starting at 8 minutes, we may miss a tag by starting to early approximately 1 out of 100 times and have to search the spots we first checked after the round started again. However being the odds are so low, its worth it to save that extra 2 minutes the vast majority of the time.

If you find a dogtag, you MUST knife the box (DO NOT SHOOT IT!!), then hit your interact button (default E) to pick up the dogtag. Be very careful around the box as just about anything will destroy it, even flares if they are within about 1 meter of the box.

Also you can only pick up a dogtag ONCE per map as each map only spawns 1 type of tag.

The dogtags will respawn during a round if they are picked up or destroyed. The respawn time if picked up is always 10 minutes. If the box is destroyed the respawn time is 11 minutes from the time the box was destroyed


2 - Assignments

You will have to unlock then complete the first three phantom assignments to be able to survive the elevator ride down to the phantom room. You unlock the first three phantom assignments by entering three passwords on Battlelog.

First go to the "Leaderboards" page on Battlelog. Near the bottom left corner of the page you will see a faded mask/skull, click on it. You will be taken to a black page with a command prompt, enter these three passwords exactly as written one at a time:

1 - bumpinthenight

2 - epic dream worlds

3 - 24344241893

Once that is done you will be able to go to the "Assignments" page on Battlelog and see what is required to complete the first three phantom assignments as well as track your progress on completing them.


3 - Elevator

The final step is preparing for your elevator ride down to the phantom room. Its simple but many people forget to do it or don't know whats required. You must equip the Final Stand map dogtag that you found in the first step as your left side dogtag and you must equip the phantom skull tag you got for completing the first phantom assignment as your right side dogtag.

Also you must equip phantom camo on either one or both of the weapons you spawn with and on your soldier.

You need at least one of every Final Stand map tag represented in the elevator to get the button to activate. Duplicate tags are ok in the elevator, they just wont help get activate it. If someone has the black memory stick dogtags from completing the fourth and last phantom assignment, they can count as 2 tags in the elevator by equipping a Final Stand map tag on the left side and their black tag on the right side.

Once your down in the phantom room there are TWO things to grab. Obviously you'll want to grab the bow but you also need to grab the orders (papers) on top of the highest part of the cabinet to the east side of the glass bow enclosure. Those papers unlock the fourth and final phantom assignment.
