Getting Final Stand Map Tags

The way we find the dogtags is to search all possible spots until we find one. This page has an excellent up to date map of the possible locations, only missing one glitched location on Hammerhead: https://imgur.com/a/hzBRN

They are in tiny boxes about the size of a box of kitchen matches. The boxes are white on Operation Whiteout and Hammerhead maps, and green with a brown lid on Giants Of Karelia and Hangar 21 maps.

The boxes will slightly glow in IRNV/PLD scopes if your within approx 40 meters of it. They do not glow at all in FLIR/MAV sights, they used to but a patch changed that.

The Final Stand dogtags can spawn at 1 of 30 (1 of 31 on Operation Whiteout) possible spots. It will ONLY spawn at 1 spot at a time. They do not spawn instantly on the start of a new round. The very first spawn in a round seems to be variable between 5-10 minutes.

We usually start looking 8 minutes after a round starts. By starting at 8 minutes, we may miss a tag by starting to early approximately 1 out of 100 times and have to search the spots we first checked after the round started again. However being the odds are so low, its worth it to save that extra 2 minutes the vast majority of the time.

If you find a dogtag, you MUST knife the box (DO NOT SHOOT IT!!), then hit your interact button (default E) to pick up the dogtag. Be very careful around the box as just about anything will destroy it, even flares if they are within about 1 meter of the box.

The dogtags will respawn during a round if they are picked up or destroyed. The respawn time if picked up is always 10 minutes. If the box is destroyed the respawn time is 11 minutes from the time the box was destroyed.
